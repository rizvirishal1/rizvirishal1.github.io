About Kerala Blasters FC

Our journey began with a vision to not just play football but to inspire and unite fans across the nation. Kerala Blasters FC has consistently showcased a commitment to excellence both on and off the field. With a rich history and a passionate fanbase, we continue to set new standards in Indian football.

Key Highlights:

  • Established in 2014
  • Competing in the Indian Super League
  • Home Ground: Jawaharlal Nehru stadium in Kochi

At Kerala Blasters FC, we believe in the power of football to transcend boundaries and bring people together. Our team comprises dedicated athletes, experienced coaching staff, and a robust support system that fuels our quest for success.

Join us on this exciting journey as we continue to strive for victory, entertain fans, and contribute to the growth of football in India. Whether you're a passionate supporter or a newcomer to the beautiful game, there's a place for you in the Kerala Blasters FC family.
